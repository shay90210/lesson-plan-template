Subject Reading & Writing
Learning Objective Students will analyze Shakespeare’s language and character choices to understand how impulsivity drives action and consequence, evaluating how timeless themes are conveyed through literary devices, dialogue, and plot. Students will examine persuasive techniques used by influential leaders and create original speeches or texts demonstrating effective rhetorical strategies. Students will analyze films as visual texts, identifying how cinematic techniques convey meaning, develop character, and communicate themes. Students will explore how poets, musicians, and spoken word artists use language and imagery to express ideas and identity, creating original works that reflect personal voice. Students will develop digital literacy skills by producing original multimedia content while applying ethical standards and audience awareness.
